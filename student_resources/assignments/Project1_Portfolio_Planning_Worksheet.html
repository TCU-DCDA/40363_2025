Part 4: Project Scope Reality Check

Keep It Simple, Keep It Excellent

Remember: This is a 3-week project with specific technical requirements. Focus on:

  • Quality over quantity - Better to have excellent content in required sections than to overwhelm yourself
  • Professional presentation - Clean, readable, accessible design trumps flashy effects
  • Required features first - Meet all technical requirements before adding extras
  • Authentic content - Be genuine about your skills and experience
